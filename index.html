How it Works

Bring your containers or use the paper bags provided……

  1. Weigh

    2. Put your container on to the scales and print out a label. The label tells us the tare weight (empty weight) of your container. Leave the sticker on for future uses.

  1. Fill

    2. Fill your container with however much you need of a single product. You can always use a funnel to help avoid spillages.

  1. Pay

    2. Take your pots full of lovely stuff to the cash desk to pay. The till will automatically deduct the weight of your container.
